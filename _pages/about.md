Hello! I am a 4th-year PhD student working with [Dr. Babak Falsafi](https://parsa.epfl.ch/~falsafi/) at [PARSA, EPFL](https://parsa.epfl.ch). I love computers, both designing and working with them. Every time I am amazed by the amount of progress we have made in ~100 years. My only wish is to contribute to the advancement of the field however I can. My current research interests include designing area- and power-efficient CPUs for server workloads. In the current post-Moore era, we must focus on making the best use of silicon and power budgets. Once upon a time, I worked on ML model compression, trying to run these huge LLMs more efficently, and also on EDA and verification.
